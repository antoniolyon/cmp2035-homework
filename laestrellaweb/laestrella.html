General Project Description

La Estrella Wind Farm project involves the development of NCRE generation plant based on the use of the wind resource in the sixth Region of Chile.
Consider the construction and subsequent commissioning and operation of up to 20 wind turbines with a total installed capacity of up to 50 MW.
The park will be connected to the grid (Sistema Interconectado Central) through a 1.6 km power transmission line to the existing 110 kV Quelentaro -Portezuelo line owned by Transnet.

Project Development

La Estrella Wind Farm project is being developed entirely by the company “Eolica La Estrella SpA”, owned by Comercial Maxagro S.A.
Comercial Maxagro is a Chilean agroindustrial company, whose main business is the pork meat production and marketing.
The land where the project (1,000Ha) is developed, is owned by Comercial Maxagro S.A., through its subsidiary Agricola Santa Lucía Ltda.

Project Assets

  • Ownership of the land where the project is developed (1,000 Ha)
  • Approved environmental permit (RCA obtained on October 2015)
  • Exploration minning concessions.
  • 82 meters high wind mast (Ammonit and Thies First Class equipment). Full data from January 2014. Resource analysis and modeling study by independent company.
  • Generation and Connection basic engineering.
  • Grid electrical studies (in progress)
  • Topography and other studies.
