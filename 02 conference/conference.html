What is the 2016 Pomaire Pottery Conference?

The 2016 Pomaire Pottery Conference will explore the personal, social and aesthetic forces that animate creative work with elemental materials, methods and ideas in the midst of the information age. The conference will promote and improve the ceramic arts through education, community-building, research and creative inspiration.
